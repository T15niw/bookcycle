FAQ

Can my small bookstore partner with you?

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with bookstores of all sizes.

Will our brand be visible if we sponsor BookCycle?

Yes, we offer brand exposure on our website, social media, events, and community reports.

Is there a minimum sponsorship requirement?

We’re flexible; both small and large contributions are valuable and acknowledged.

Do I need to commit for a long time as a volunteer?

No long-term commitment is required, join us when you can, every little bit helps!

Do I need previous experience to volunteer?

Not at all! We'll guide you based on your interests and skills.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Some roles like content creation administrative assistance can be done remotely. The other roles are local.

Don’t hesitate to reach out through our contact form!
